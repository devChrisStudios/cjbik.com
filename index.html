What is BIKFAM?

More than just stickers

BIKFAM is the community built around CJBIK's YouTube channel. We're riders, builders, and enthusiasts who live for the ride — whether that's on an e-bike, a motorcycle, or pushing pedals up a mountain trail.

These stickers are our way of bringing the community together. Slap one on your gear and you're instantly part of the fam. Spot another BIKFAM sticker in the wild? That's your people.
